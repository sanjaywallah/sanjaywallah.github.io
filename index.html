About Me

I am a multi-talented, curious, passionate, risk taker. Uniting my passions for art, design, and the Web is what I want to do with the rest of my life.

After earning my first Bachelor’s degree, I embarked on a successful career as a paralegal, as I had wished to become a lawyer. But after working in corporate immigration law for years, I still did not know what I wished to do. Then a few years ago, I discovered art and design, so I decided to go back to school to study Graphic Design and Studio Arts.

This May, I earned Bachelor degrees in Graphic Design and Studio Art. With no previous background in Art or Design, I initially struggled, but my curiosity, passion, love of learning, and perseverance held me in good stead. Then in my junior year, I took a course in web design, and I fell in love. But my school offered no classes in modern Web languages and technologies, so soon after graduation, I enrolled in General Assembly’s 13 week Web Development Immersive Bootcamp, which I successfully completed in September.

I am proficient in the Adobe Design Suite, and I have good knowledge of JavaScript, AngularJS, React, Ruby, and Ruby on Rails.

Developer Projects

This Tower of Hanoi game is implemented in JavaScript. This well-known math game consists of three rods and a number of disks of different sizes, which can slide onto any rod. The objective of the game is to move the entire stack of discs from the first rod to the last according to a simple set of rules.

The "Cheerups" site allows users to create, share, and up vote Cheer Ups. This Ruby on Rails site provides full CRUD functionality, so users can create, read, update, and delete their own Cheer Ups. The site also employs various validations and authentication processes to prevent potential malicious actions.

This site is built on Rails and AngularJS. It allows users to select from a list of available memes or create their own. Users can comment on memes, search for memes. generate random memes, and view a history of the memes they created. The site provides full CRUD functionality for memes and comments.
